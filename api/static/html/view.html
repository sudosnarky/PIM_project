Projects Created: September 15, 2025

Introduction

This is a sample note demonstrating the PARA method for organizing information. The PARA method consists of four main categories:

The Four Categories

  • Projects - Things that have a deadline and specific outcome
  • Areas - Ongoing responsibilities to maintain over time
  • Resources - Topics or themes of ongoing interest
  • Archives - Items from the other three categories that are no longer active

Key Benefits

The PARA method helps you:

  1. Organize information based on actionability
  2. Reduce cognitive load by having clear categories
  3. Find information quickly when you need it
  4. Move seamlessly between planning and execution

"The goal of organizing information is not to make it look neat, but to make it useful for completing projects and moving your life forward." - Tiago Forte

For more information, visit the official PARA method guide.
